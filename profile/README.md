
# Tiger CRM: Open Source Developer Management

Tiger CRM: Open Source Developer Management system is designed to streamline the identification, organization, sponsorship, and management of key open-source developers on behalf of Tiger CRM's clients. This ensures clients maximize their impact, engagement, and return on investment from strategic open-source contributions and community support.

## OSDM Features

- A single dedicated point of contact who will manage the entire lifecycle of a sponsored open-source developer relationship:

- Developer Profiles: Detailed tracking of developer contributions, project focus, contact information, and sponsorship history.

- Client-Sponsorship Mapping: Clear linkage between specific client funds/goals and the sponsored developers.

- Impact Metrics: Tracking and reports on developer activity, project impact, and client visibility generated by the sponsorship.

- Financial Tracking: Management of sponsorship payments, grants, and stipends.

- Communication Log: Centralized logging of all correspondence, agreements, and engagement notes.
